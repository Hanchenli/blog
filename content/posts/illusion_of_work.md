In the past few decades, we have been creating the illusion of "higher" work. With the rise of automation and artificial intelligence, many jobs that were once considered essential have been replaced by machines. This has led to a shift in the labor market, where more and more people are finding themselves in jobs that require less skill and offer lower wages.

Ideas:
higher work were both 
1. moat of capitalists, shibboleths for social status.
2. how economies has grown over the years to reinforce, the new upper middle class people mostly do intellectual work.

However, this illusion of higher work is no longer true. 
Search is relatively simple. The shibboleth or moat dissolves. That is why our workers are anxious. They are losing their social status. (they could always be doing plumbering?)

Economy will probably shift to a more idea driven world.
